What is the best suitable time to visit India?

The best time to visit India starts from October till April, however in order to avail the off season discounts you can contact us for the remaining period of the year. Also, the suitable time depends upon the location you are planning to visit.

Is it safe to make payment online with Max Holidays?

Yes, you can truly count on us for making the payment online with Max Holidays. Our online payment method is completely safe. Max Holidays’ highly authentic payment gateway does not save any of your credit/debit card’s information. Your money is safe with us!

Is it reliable to book a tour with Max Holidays?

Yes, it is completely safe to book a tour with us as all the details are very clearly mentioned on the website. If you still have queries, we are just a call or a mail away. You can contact us at 011-40504050 or email us at

enquiry@maxholidays.com
Is it safe for women to travel solo to India?

Yes, it is completely safe for women to travel solo to India, as our tour representative or an accompanying tour guide will always be there throughout the journey. He/she will be acting informing you about each and every bit that comes your way.

Is your company registered with Ministry of India?

Yes, we are registered with Ministry of India & also are eminently recognised as an IATO Authorized Company. Apart from that, ours is an ISO 9001:2018 Quality Standard certified organization and also, we have been awarded as the Best Asia Pacific Company.

Does your company works completely online?

Max Holidays work online & offline both, as you can book your trip by making payment online, but before or after making the payment also, you can reach our team at any time just in case you want any kind of services.
